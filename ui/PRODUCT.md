# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Supplier operations staff who process retailer orders and invoices. They need to keep documents moving quickly and confidently without prior EDI knowledge or dependence on technical specialists for routine work.

## Product Purpose

EDI Spark gives supplier operations teams one clear workspace for retailer EDI. It helps them process retailer orders quickly, maintain complete visibility across orders, invoices, and responses, and resolve exceptions before they delay fulfilment.

Success means routine documents move with minimal manual effort, exceptions surface early with an understandable next step, and staff can confidently determine the status of every exchange.

## Positioning

Retailer EDI without the traditional overhead: a clear operational workspace that makes complex retailer connections manageable for people who do not speak EDI.

## Brand Personality

Clear, calm, and capable. The product communicates in plain English, creates confidence without noise, and demonstrates technical depth only when the task requires it.

## Anti-references

The dashboard must not feel like dense traditional enterprise EDI software, overly technical developer tooling, or a generic SaaS dashboard. It should avoid jargon-first workflows, visual clutter, obscure statuses, and interchangeable card-based layouts.

## Design Principles

1. **Lead with the next action.** Make document state, urgency, and the appropriate response immediately understandable.
2. **Explain issues in plain English.** Translate EDI complexity into clear operational language while preserving technical detail on demand.
3. **Keep every document traceable.** Make status, history, relationships, and outcomes easy to follow from arrival through delivery.
4. **Expose complexity progressively.** Keep routine workflows simple and reveal raw messages, mappings, and integration detail only when useful.
5. **Make routine processing fast.** Reduce portal hopping, re-keying, repetitive decisions, and unnecessary navigation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support complete keyboard operation, clearly visible focus, semantic screen-reader navigation, sufficient text and interface contrast, status communication that does not rely on colour alone, comfortable target sizes, and reduced-motion preferences.
